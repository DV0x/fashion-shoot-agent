# Fuji Velvia Style Block

A film emulation style for high-fashion editorial photography. Creates a distinctive, saturated, grainy aesthetic.

---

## Style Block (Copy This)

```
The image is shot on Fuji Velvia film with a hard flash, the light is concentrated on the subject and fades slightly toward the edges of the frame. The image is overexposed showing significant film grain and is oversaturated. The skin appears shiny (almost oily), and there are harsh white reflections on accessories.

3:2 aspect ratio
```

---

## Extended Style Block (with lens spec)

```
The image is shot on Fuji Velvia film on a 55mm prime lens with a hard flash, the light is concentrated on the subject and fades slightly toward the edges of the frame. The image is overexposed showing significant film grain and is oversaturated. The skin appears shiny (almost oily), and there are harsh white reflections on accessories.

3:2 aspect ratio
```

---

## Style Parameters

| Parameter | Value | Effect |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| **Film Stock** | Fuji Velvia | High saturation, vibrant colors |
| **Exposure** | Overexposed | Bright highlights, lifted shadows |
| **Grain** | Significant | Textured, organic feel |
| **Saturation** | Oversaturated | Punchy, editorial colors |
| **Skin Rendering** | Shiny/oily | Wet look, fashion aesthetic |
| **Reflections** | Harsh white | Specular highlights on glass/metal |
| **Vignetting** | Natural falloff | Light fades toward edges |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 3:2 | Standard fashion format |

---

## Lighting Characteristics

### Hard Flash Setup
- **Key light:** Hard flash (not diffused)
- **Quality:** Concentrated on subject
- **Falloff:** Fades toward frame edges
- **Shadows:** Sharp, defined

### Natural Vignette
The light concentration creates organic vignetting without post-processing.

---

## When to Use

- High-fashion editorial shoots
- Contact sheet generation
- Hero image generation
- Frame isolation (maintain consistency)
- Any frame in the Tim workflow

---

## Variations

### Warmer Velvia
Add "with warm color temperature" for golden tones.

### Cooler Velvia
Add "with cool blue undertones" for fashion-forward look.

### Higher Grain
Add "with heavy film grain" for more textured aesthetic.

### Less Overexposed
Replace "overexposed" with "slightly overexposed" for more detail retention.

## Alternative Style Blocks

### Kodak Portra (Softer Alternative)
```
The image is shot on Kodak Portra 400 film with soft natural lighting. The colors are muted and natural with subtle pastel tones. Skin appears smooth with natural texture. Soft film grain throughout.

3:2 aspect ratio
```

### High Contrast B&W
```
The image is shot on Ilford HP5 black and white film with harsh directional lighting. Deep blacks and bright whites with minimal midtones. Strong film grain and high contrast editorial look.

3:2 aspect ratio
```

### Digital Clean
```
The image is shot with a modern digital medium format camera. Colors are accurate and vibrant. Skin is smooth with natural texture. Sharp throughout with no grain. Professional studio lighting.

3:2 aspect ratio
```
